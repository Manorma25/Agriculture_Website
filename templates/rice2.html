About Rice Production

Rice is a staple food for more than half of the world's population, especially in Asia. Its production is highly dependent on water availability, resulting to changes in rainfall patterns and drought. Irrigation techniques have improved rice production, but challenges such as climate change and other factors continue to threaten sustainable production.
