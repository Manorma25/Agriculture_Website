Potassium Production

Potassium plays a vital role in agriculture as an essential nutrient that regulates plant water balance, enzyme activation, and photosynthesis. It is commonly sourced from potassium-bearing minerals, with potash being the primary form used in fertilizers. Potassium helps improve crop q uality, drought resistance, and disease tolerance. Efficient potassium management is key to boosting agricultural productivity while maintaining soil health.
